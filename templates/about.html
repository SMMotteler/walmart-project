About Cottagecore for All

This website was created for the 2021 Walmart X Girls Who Code Grace Hopper Scholarship. This website imagines a new feature for Walmart that would allow them to reach out to a younger group of customers who value interpersonal interactions and want to explore new styles of clothing and decor. This feature would take the form of a sort of social media platform where customers could create profiles and curate collections of different Walmart products or projects using Walmart products.
